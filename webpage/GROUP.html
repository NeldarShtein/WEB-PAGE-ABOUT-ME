GROUP

I was and I am the manager of a community of people. Although initially it all started as a small group for people who like anime, but eventually the group became more popular and more and more people began to join.
We gladly accepted everyone in our ranks and we always had a friendly atmosphere. Previously, gatherings were held more often, but we still organize them, albeit in an online format.
