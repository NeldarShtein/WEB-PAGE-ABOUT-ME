HAIR

I like long hair. At one time, I even decided to grow my hair. As a result, they grew so much that I was already collecting them in the tail, as they interfered with me, blocking everything in front of my eyes. As a result, I realized that long hair does not suit me, so now I have it so long that it does not get into my eyes and does not stand, as after being hit by electricity.
