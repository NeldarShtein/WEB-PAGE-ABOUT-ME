LIEPAJA

I was born and lived for 20 years in a city on the shores of the Baltic Sea. In Liepaja, the weather is quite similar to English, so I did not experience acclimatization. Only here in my city, a strong wind blows almost all the time, so it was nicknamed the city of seven winds.
