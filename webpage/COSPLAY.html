COSPLAY

Every summer I attended an event in Riga.
This event is called Unicon and it is dedicated to the themes of games, TV series, films, anime, as well as recent years and K-pop.
It was great to come in and watch the quality cosplays and basically have the opportunity to take pictures with characters from different fandoms.
