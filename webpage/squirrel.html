SQUIRREL

Squirrels are the animals that I like the most. Every time I go to London, I definitely visit the park at Buckingham Palace, having previously bought nuts to feed the squirrels.
Once around 10 squirrels gathered around me at one moment. I also occasionally go to Nottingham Parks to feed and watch squirrels.
